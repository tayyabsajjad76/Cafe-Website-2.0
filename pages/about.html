Since 2014

A Café Built on Curiosity

Aurum began when two friends — a barista and a pastry chef — asked a simple question: what if every detail of the café experience felt considered?

We opened on Willow Lane with twelve seats, a single-group espresso machine, and a commitment to roast only what we could serve at its peak. Guests came for the coffee and stayed for the calm.

Today we still mill flour for our laminated doughs, cup new lots every week, and design spaces that invite you to slow down — because luxury, to us, is time well spent.

Guiding Lights

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To serve specialty coffee and artisan food with warmth and precision — creating a place where craftsmanship and hospitality meet every guest equally.

Vision

To be the city's most trusted café for people who value quality without spectacle — a quiet landmark of taste, care, and community.

Values

Integrity in sourcing, generosity in service, restraint in design, and a relentless focus on the cup and the plate before anything else.

Milestones

Our Journey

2014

Doors Open on Willow Lane

Twelve seats, one espresso machine, and a pastry case that sold out by noon every Saturday.

2017

In-House Roasting Begins

We installed our first 15kg roaster and began publishing origin notes with every bag.

2020

Expanded Dining Room

Doubled capacity while protecting the quiet atmosphere guests loved — soft lighting, warm oak, no rush.

2024

Aurum Tasting Studio

Launched private cuppings and bakery workshops for guests who want to go deeper into the craft.
